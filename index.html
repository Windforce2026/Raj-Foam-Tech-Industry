Common Questions

Is this suitable for post-surgery recovery?

Yes, the Ortho-Core series provides the rigid stability often required after spinal procedures. However, we always recommend consulting your surgeon first.

How does HA10 differ from memory foam?

Standard memory foam sinks and traps heat. HA10 is a high-resilience polymer that pushes back against weight to keep the spine neutral and has open cells for cooling.

Can I claim medical insurance?

Spinelife mattresses are classified as orthopedic aids. Check with your insurance provider regarding coverage for prescribed medical devices.
